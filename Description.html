Now I need a Pink P90. Me after seeing LLENN's gun. Well, this is my attempt to recreate the HUD present during Squad Jam in Gun Gale Online. Is not perfect yet, but is a Work in Progress. Prerequisites
  • GTA V 1.0.231.0 (After Hours) or higher (required)
  • ScriptHookV 1.0.1493.0 or higher (required)
  • ScriptHookVDotNet 2.10.6 or higher (required)
  • GTA V FoV (recommended)
  • Pink P90 and Skorpion (recommended)
Install
  • Put everything that is on the 7z file in your *scripts* folder
